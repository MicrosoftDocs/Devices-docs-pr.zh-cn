### <span data-ttu-id="4f562-108">手动注册</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f562-108">Manual registration</span></span>

   ![使用 Intune 注册 Surface Hub 2S](images/sh2-set-intune1.png)<br>
   
> [!NOTE]
