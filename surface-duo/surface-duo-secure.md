<span data-ttu-id="d2682-104">Surface Duo 在具有深入集成的硬件、固件和软件的每一层内置保护，以保持设备、标识和数据的安全。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-104">Surface Duo has protection built in at every layer with deeply integrated hardware, firmware, and software to keep your devices, identities, and data secure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2682-105">作为 Android 10 设备，Surface Duo 在操作系统级别和 Google 服务层利用 Android 安全功能。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-105">As an Android 10 device, Surface Duo utilizes Android security features at the OS level and at the Google services layer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2682-106">Android 操作系统利用传统操作系统安全控件来保护用户数据和系统资源，保护设备完整性免受恶意软件的攻击，并提供应用程序隔离。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-106">The Android OS leverages traditional OS security controls to protect user data and system resources, protects device integrity against malware, and provides application isolation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2682-107">此外，Google 还提供一些分层在操作系统顶部的服务，这些服务与 Android 操作系统安全性结合使用时，有助于持续保护 Android 用户。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-107">Additionally, Google provides a number of services layered on top of the OS that, when combined with Android OS security, help to continuously protect the Android user.</span></span>

- **<span data-ttu-id="d2682-108">自定义工程 UEFI。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-108">Custom engineered UEFI.</span></span>** <span data-ttu-id="d2682-109">Surface Duo 在 Android 设备中的独特之处是 Microsoft 自定义设计的统一可扩展固件接口 (UEFI) ，可完全控制固件组件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-109">Unique to Surface Duo, among Android devices, is Microsoft’s custom engineered Unified Extensible Firmware Interface (UEFI) which enables full control over firmware components.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2682-110">Microsoft 通过编写或审阅内部的每一行固件代码，为 Surface Duo 提供企业级安全性，从而使 Microsoft 能够直接敏捷地响应潜在固件威胁，并降低供应链安全风险。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-110">Microsoft delivers Enterprise-grade security to Surface Duo by writing or reviewing every line of firmware code in house, enabling Microsoft to respond directly and agilely, to potential firmware threats and to mitigate supply chain security risks.</span></span>
- **<span data-ttu-id="d2682-111">已验证启动。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-111">Verified Boot.</span></span>** <span data-ttu-id="d2682-112">从登录后的硬件级别开始，验证启动努力确保执行的代码仅来自受信任的源。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-112">Starting at the hardware level upon sign-in, Verified Boot strives to ensure executed code only comes from a trusted source.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2682-113">它建立一个完整的信任链 - 从受硬件保护的信任根到启动加载程序、启动分区和其他已验证分区。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-113">It establishes a full chain of trust -- from hardware-protected root of trust to the bootloader, boot partition and other verified partitions.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d2682-114">当 Surface Duo 启动时，每个阶段将验证下一阶段的完整性和真实性，然后再进行执行。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-114">When Surface Duo boots up, each stage verifies the integrity and authenticity of the next stage before handing over execution.</span></span>

- **<span data-ttu-id="d2682-119">Google Play Protect。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-119">Google Play Protect.</span></span>** <span data-ttu-id="d2682-120">在软件层，Surface Duo 使用 Google Play 保护威胁检测来扫描所有应用程序，包括 Google Play 中的公共应用、Microsoft 和运营商更新的系统应用以及旁加载的应用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d2682-120">At the software layer, Surface Duo uses Google Play Protect threat detection, which scans all applications including public apps from Google Play, system apps updated by Microsoft and carriers, and sideloaded apps.</span></span>
